The Rules of Wards and Firewalls


Player 1 plays as the Castle Guards

Player 2 plays as the Thieves Guild

The Guards protect the Castle, while the Thief Guild wants to destroy it

The Guards win once the Castle has survived for 20 turns

The thieves win when an attack turns the Castle bankrupt

Each team automatically earns some money every turn

You have the option to draw cards or play the cards you have

Beware. Some cards need access to the castle to be played
