Playing as a Guard

You see 4 Options in your Menu

Each turn, you draw or play a card

At the beginning of each round, you earn some amount of Gold

The whims of Lady Luck could lead to your glory or your downfall



When you click on 'Draw Card', you draw a random card from the deck

You may draw Duplicates, but they help you maintain your upgrades

Your turn is Completed once you have drawn a Card




When you click on 'See Real Life Equivalents', you can see which method of cyber defence each card represents




Clicking 'See Card Countdown' reveals the number of turns your acquired upgrades will remain active

The Cost Multiplier directly increases the buying cost; for example, a 1.5 multiplier makes a 60-cost item become 90

The Exfiltration Multiplier similarly boosts the amount of money Thieves successfully steal from the Castle




Clicking 'Play Card' lets you play one of the cards you have drawn




If you do not have enough Gold to play a Drawn card, you can play another Card or Draw a fresh card




If you have been wise with your Budget, you can play a Card to upgrade your defence

Upgrades are essential in protecting your Castle from the Thieves Guild

Each upgrade protects you for 5 turns before becoming obsolete

If you draw a duplicate card, you can play it again to maintain the upgrade at a cheaper price, extending the upgrade for 5 more turns

But upgrades are earned by answering a question based on the cards' real life equivalents



Answering the question correctly, earns you the upgrade

Rome was not built in a day, learn about these upgrades at your own pace



If your answer is wrong, don't worry, there is always the next time

But for this turn, you miss out on your upgrade and your Gold



Now that you have the basics down, look at the cards drawn by the Thieves during their turn

Predict the time when the Thieves might play their cards and counter them to protect your Castle

Luck is a decisive factor here, but you can still forge the destiny of your Castle

If your Castle goes bankrupt, you lose



Protect your Castle for 20 Turns and you Win

The Thieves begin to grow paranoid, fight internally and leave the Kingdom
