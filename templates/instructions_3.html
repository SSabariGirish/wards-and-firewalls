Playing as a Thief

You see 4 Options in your Menu

Just like the guards, each turn, you draw or play a card

At the beginning of each round, you earn some amount of Gold

The whims of Lady Luck could lead to your glory or your downfall



When you click on 'Draw Card', you draw a random card from the deck

You may draw Duplicates, but they help you execute an attack once again

Your turn is Completed once you have drawn a Card




When you click on 'See Real Life Equivalents', you can see which method of cyber attack each card represents




Clicking 'Play Card' lets you play one of the cards you have drawn




If you do not have enough Gold to play a Drawn card, you can play another Card or Draw a fresh card




If you have been wise with your Budget, you can play a Card to attack the Castle

Attacks are essential in causing chaos within the Castle and keeping the Guards on their toes

Some attacks can be initiated outside the Castle, while some explicitly require access to the Castle

If your attack does not land, the Guards might become aware, and render the card useless for a few turns

An attack is successfully initiated by rolling a D20 die



If you roll more than or equal to the mentioned number, you move to answering the question

Otherwise, your attack is directly deemed unsuccessful







Answering the question correctly, allows you to successfully execute the attack

Rome was not built in a day, learn about these dangerous cyber attacks at your own pace



If your answer is wrong, don't worry, there is always the next time

But for this turn, you miss out on your attack, your Gold and will initiate the applicable cooldowns on the card



Clicking 'See Card Countdown' reveals the number of turns before you can attempt a previously failed attack

Each successful attack registers a Weakness in the Castle

These Weaknesses can be used to in the 'Brigands and Bandits' Card




Now that you have the basics down, look at the cards drawn by the Guards during their turn

Predict the time when the Guards might play their cards and counter them to ransack the Castle

Luck is a decisive factor here, but you can still forge the destiny of your Guild

If the Castle goes bankrupt, you Win!

The Castle is on fire and the Royal Family escapes



If the Castle remains standing for 20 Turns, you Lose
